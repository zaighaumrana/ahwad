Privacy Policy

How Ahwad collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Ahwad ("we", "us", "our"), a modest fashion brand operated from Islamabad, Pakistan, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. By using ahwad.com or registering on our waitlist, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Business Name: Ahwad
Operating From: Islamabad, Pakistan
Contact: founders.ahwad@gmail.com
Instagram: @ahwadpk

2. Legal Framework

This policy is drafted in accordance with: Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016 (Pakistan); Pakistan Personal Data Protection Bill principles; GDPR principles for EU/UK users; UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 for UAE users; and CAN-SPAM Act principles for US users.

3. Information We Collect

When you register on our waitlist, we collect your name, email address, country, and city. We also collect standard technical data automatically: browser type, operating system, pages viewed, referring website, and anonymised IP address. We do not collect payment information, national identity numbers, or any sensitive personal data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To notify you when Ahwad officially launches
  • To send early access information and exclusive pre-launch previews
  • To understand our geographic audience
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We will never sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Consent — you voluntarily submit your information. You may withdraw consent at any time. Legitimate Interest — to operate and improve our website. Legal Obligation — where required by applicable law.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information. We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies strictly necessary for the website to function. We do not currently use advertising cookies, Google Analytics, social media pixels, or behavioural advertising tools.

8. Your Rights

  • Right of Access — to request a copy of the personal data we hold
  • Right to Rectification — to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure — to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Object — to object to processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — to unsubscribe at any time

To exercise any right, email founders.ahwad@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance.
