Privacy Policy

How Ahwad collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Ahwad ("we", "us", "our"), a modest fashion brand operated from Islamabad, Pakistan, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. By using our website at ahwad.com or registering on our waitlist, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Business Name: Ahwad
Operating From: Islamabad, Pakistan
Contact Email: founders.ahwad@gmail.com
Instagram: @ahwadpk

Ahwad is a modest fashion brand for Muslim women in Pakistan and the Middle East. We are currently in pre-launch phase and operate a waitlist registration service at this website.

2. Legal Framework

This policy is drafted in accordance with:

  • Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016 — Pakistan's primary legislation governing electronic data and cybercrime
  • Pakistan Telecommunication (Re-organisation) Act 1996
  • Personal Data Protection Bill (Pakistan) — we comply with its principles in anticipation of its enactment
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles — applied as international best practice for users in the EU and UK
  • CAN-SPAM Act principles — for email communications to users in the United States
  • UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on personal data protection, for users in the UAE

3. Information We Collect

When you register on our waitlist, we collect:

  • Your name — to personalise our communications with you
  • Your email address — to send you launch notifications and updates
  • Your country and city — to understand our geographic audience and tailor our launch accordingly

We also collect standard technical data automatically when you visit our website:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages viewed and time spent on site
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • IP address (anonymised where possible)

We do not collect payment information, national identity numbers, or any sensitive personal data such as religious beliefs, health information, or biometric data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To notify you when Ahwad officially launches
  • To send you early access information and exclusive pre-launch previews if you have registered for the waitlist
  • To understand our audience geographically so we can prioritise launch markets
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We will never sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent — you voluntarily submit your information to join our waitlist. You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing us.
  • Legitimate Interest — to operate and improve our website and understand our audience
  • Legal Obligation — where required by Pakistani law or applicable international regulation

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Currently, waitlist data is managed by Ahwad's founding team and is not shared with any third-party data processors. Should we adopt third-party email or CRM services in the future, we will update this policy accordingly and notify all registered users.

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies strictly necessary for the website to function correctly. We do not currently use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party analytics platforms (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Behavioural advertising tools

If we introduce any analytics or tracking tools in the future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notice and consent mechanisms.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access — to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten") — to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Object — to object to our processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — to unsubscribe or remove yourself from our waitlist at any time
  • Right to Data Portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (for EU/UK users)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at founders.ahwad@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data to us, please contact us immediately and we will delete the information promptly.

10. Social Media

Our website contains links to our social media profiles on Facebook, Instagram, and Threads. When you visit these platforms, their own privacy policies govern the collection and use of your data. We are not responsible for the data practices of third-party platforms. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed primarily in Pakistan. If any of our service providers process data outside Pakistan, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or operational needs. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Ahwad
Email: founders.ahwad@gmail.com
Location: Islamabad, Pakistan
Instagram: @ahwadpk

We are committed to resolving any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
